Story of Three PIGS

Once upon a time there were three little pigs living in a forest. They were living happily.

One fine day there came a wolf looking a prey to fulfill it's hunger. It went on looking looking... looking.

In order to protect themselves the pigs deceide to build a house. So each pig decided to build a house of their own

Houses built by the pigs

Knowing that one of the pig made house of straw which is a weak material. The pigs decided to fool the wolf since the wolf would first try for straw house. They filled the straw house with honey bees.

The wolf came searching for the pigs. It approached the straw house. But ended up returning with swellings.
